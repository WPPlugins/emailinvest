Easy add an email subscription form to your Wordpress website and start collect users data as emails, names or birthdays and grow your email list. Use them in your Emailinvest account. Send and track your email campaigns.


== Description ==

This plugin will help you collect the emails of your subscribers by adding a subscription form to your Wordpress website! It allows you to connect your subscribers with your Emailinvest software. You can even manage more than one subscription form, select how te form appears and put your your own title to it!



== Installation ==

1. Upload the EmailInvest plugin to your blog, Activate it.
2. Use WIDGET to place your form.
3. Type [add_form id=ID w=350px] where you want to show your subscription form. To add a form in your post (where ID is your form ID from Emailinvest account, w and h are width and height)
